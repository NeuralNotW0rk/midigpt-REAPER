We also included in our training dataset a number of MIDI files found on the web that were marked as being Public Domain, marked as available under a Creative Commons CC0 license, or otherwise marked as being free to use without attribution.
We took care to exclude MIDI files from our training dataset that contained copyrighted music that we knew were incorrectly marked (e.g., remixes of copyrighted music, the Super Mario Bros Main Theme, etc.).

All MIDI files referenced in this document were radically transformed for purposes of this project, from their original states into a set of learned model weights in binary form.
